# HD SEO Local Growth Engine

The Local Growth Engine turns the strongest competitive patterns into one evidence-controlled system. It is not a bulk AI article generator and it does not promise rankings.

## Product surfaces

- **Brand DNA** gives every agent a durable, tenant-scoped voice, audience, vocabulary, and style profile. Style is never accepted as factual proof.
- **Local Growth Plan** mixes technical, internal-link, refresh, local, conversion, AI-visibility, authority, and selective content work over 30, 60, and 90 days. A verified service area is required and all plan items pause for approval.
- **Internal-link graph** records observed and broken links, detects orphaned owner pages, and proposes semantically relevant contextual links.
- **Refresh intelligence** compares two 28-day Search Console windows and crawl evidence so an existing owner page is improved before overlapping content is created.
- **AI visibility** tracks verified observations for local buyer questions across Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and other engines. It never treats a mention as caused by HD SEO without evidence.
- **Earned authority** scores legitimate local, editorial, supplier, association, sponsorship, data, expert, and mention-reclamation opportunities. Link exchanges and paid dofollow schemes are rejected server-side.
- **Interactive tools** specify useful calculators, checkers, estimators, comparisons, generators, decision trees, and guides. Calculators and estimators remain evidence-gated when verified claims are missing.
- **Outcome snapshots** package Search Console, lead, and execution evidence with explicit confounders for human verification.
- **Public audit** uses the production SSRF-protected crawler, a 25-page limit, and rate limiting to produce a transparent technical snapshot and route qualified prospects into onboarding.

## Production setup

1. Apply migrations through `0021_local_growth_engine.sql` in numerical order.
2. Regenerate Supabase types after the migration is applied.
3. Confirm the background evidence workers are healthy so crawl and Search Console rows stay fresh.
4. Complete the client business profile, active services, and service areas before generating a roadmap.
5. Review and approve the roadmap. Publishing, spend, DNS, destructive changes, and unsupported claims remain protected by the existing approval and rollback control planes.

The agency portal exposes the workspace at **Local Growth**. The public audit is available at `/audit`.
